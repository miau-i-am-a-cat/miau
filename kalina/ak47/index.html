The AK-47 Bag is the flagship piece of KALINA's Signature Collection. Born from the intersection of provocation and craftsmanship, it challenges the conventions of luxury accessories while maintaining impeccable quality.

Sculpture Meets Function

Every KALINA piece begins as a vision — a collision of art and utility. The AK-47 Bag emerged from a simple question: What if the most recognizable weapon became a symbol of creative defiance?

Our artisans work exclusively with leather sourced from a single Italian tannery, aged for eighteen months before cutting. The relief is created through a proprietary molding technique developed over years of experimentation.
