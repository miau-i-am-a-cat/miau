    How It Works

    Melatonin is your body's "darkness signal." Taken before bed, it advances the brain's timing for sleep and can shorten the time it takes to fall asleep. Effects are modest but consistent across meta-analyses, and safest when used to cue timing rather than as a heavy sedative.

    Synergy in This Formula

    Pairs with calming botanicals to smooth pre-sleep arousal; B6 supports the natural serotoninmelatonin pathway.

    Natural Occurrence

    Your pineal gland makes melatonin nightly; trace "phytomelatonin" also exists in plants.

    FAQ Section - Wingman Sleep

    Sleep Strip FAQ

    How fast will I fall asleep?

    The strip dissolves in under a minute and delivers melatonin directly through your mouth for faster absorption than pills or gummies. Take it about 1530 minutes before you want to sleep, in a dark environment with your phone put away. Most people feel relaxed and ready for sleep within 1530 minutes.

    Will I wake up groggy?

    Most users don't. The grogginess people experience with melatonin gummies often comes from the sugar and additives in those products not the melatonin itself. Our strips have zero sugar and no unnecessary fillers. Plus, we include 5 calming botanicals (valerian, chamomile, passionflower, lavender, and vitamin B6) that help you wind down naturally and sleep better so you wake up refreshed, not foggy.

    Is it habit-forming?

    No. None of the ingredients are habit-forming. Melatonin is a hormone your body already produces naturally we're just giving it a nudge. The other ingredients are natural botanicals (valerian, chamomile, passionflower, lavender) that have been used for centuries to support relaxation. There's no dependence or withdrawal. Use it when you need it.

    What if I wake up in the middle of the night?

    Wingman Sleep helps you fall asleep faster. Most users sleep through the night, especially when combined with good sleep hygiene dark room, cool temperature, no screens. Note: the max is 1 strip per 24 hours. If you frequently wake at night, it may be worth looking at other factors like stress, caffeine timing, or room environment.

    What's in it?

    5mg melatonin, 25mg valerian root, 10mg chamomile, 10mg passionflower, 10mg lavender, and 1.7mg Vitamin B6. Cranberry-mint flavor. Zero sugar, zero calories naturally sweetened with monk fruit and stevia. Made with plant-based pullulan film. Free from major allergens and gluten. Made in FDA-registered, GMP-certified facilities.

    Who shouldn't use it?

    Sleep strips aren't for everyone. Avoid if you're: under 18, pregnant or nursing. Do not take with alcohol or sedatives. Do not drive or operate machinery after taking. If you're on SSRIs, immunosuppressants, hormone therapies, or any medication that affects sleep, consult your doctor first.

    How do I use it?

    Peel the sachet from the top and place the strip in your mouth. Let it dissolve takes about 30 seconds. No water needed. Take it 1530 minutes before bed. Pro tip: place it under your tongue for faster absorption. And if it sticks to the roof of your mouth, don't worry it will work no matter where in your mouth it sticks to.
