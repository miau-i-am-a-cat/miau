Date October 26, 2024 To You — the founder, the builder, the one who can't sleep From Your Wingman Re: What is your job?
To whom it may concern —1

You make the product. You serve the customer. You win the market. You have enough to worry about. We know, because we've watched founders like you try to do everything — and burn through time, money, and momentum in the process.

That is not your job. Your job is the thing only you can do. The vision. The product. The impossible decisions at 2AM. Everything else — the identity, the system, the story the world sees — that is our job.

§

We build brands end to end. Strategy through execution. One team, every discipline, in-house. No contractors. No freelancers. No telephone game between six different vendors who've never met. One mind. One system. One outcome.

The result is a brand that looks, feels, and performs like it was built by a single hand — because it was. Coherent down to the pixel. Considered down to the comma. The kind of brand that makes people stop and ask, who did this?

Yours in the system,
the wingman project
Brand Studio — Los Angeles, CA
