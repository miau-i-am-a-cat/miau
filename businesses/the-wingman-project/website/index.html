Our Approach

Not another
creative agency.

We don't do "deliverables" and "stakeholder meetings." We build brands that make money. Period. Our process is fast, opinionated, and results-obsessed.

01

Discovery

We dig deep into who you are, who your audience is, and what makes you different. No 47-page brand audits — just real conversations.

02

Strategy

Market positioning, product selection, pricing architecture, and conversion strategy. We build the blueprint before we touch a pixel.

03

Identity

Logo, typography, color, photography direction — a complete visual system that's unmistakably you. Designed to work everywhere.

04

Build

We design and develop your entire e-commerce experience. Every page, every product, every pixel optimized for conversion.

05

Launch

Go live with confidence. We handle the technical details, integrate your tools, and make sure day one runs smooth.

06

Grow

Ongoing optimization, new product launches, seasonal campaigns. We're partners in your success, not just vendors.

About Us

Built different.

The Wingman Project was founded in Los Angeles with a simple belief: creators deserve better.

We watched talented people struggle to monetize their influence — settling for generic merch, overpriced agencies, or doing it themselves badly. There had to be a better way.

So we built it. A studio that combines world-class design, technical expertise, and e-commerce know-how to launch complete brands in weeks. We're not here to hand you a logo and wish you luck. We're here to build something that actually makes money.

Our team includes former leads from top design studios, e-commerce veterans who've scaled brands to 8 figures, and technologists who obsess over conversion rates. We're selective about who we work with — but when we commit, we go all in.
