## Legal Structure

### Equity Agreement
- **Split:** 51% Wingman Project / 49% Influencer
- **Vesting:** Influencer equity vests over 12 months
- **Buyout Clause:** Either party can buy out at 3x trailing 12-month profit
- **IP:** Brand assets owned by the entity, licensed to influencer for content

### Entity Structure
- Each brand = separate LLC (liability isolation)
- The Wingman Project = holding company (51% member of each LLC)
- Influencer = 49% member of their brand's LLC

### Compliance
- FTC disclosure requirements for influencer promotion
- Product liability insurance per brand
- Terms of service / privacy policy templates
- Supplier agreements with quality guarantees
- FDA compliance for supplements (suppliers handle)
